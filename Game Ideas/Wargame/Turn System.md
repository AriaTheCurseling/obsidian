
Take turns activating or using a unit. To activate a unit, roll its action dice and mark it "activated". To use a unit, choose an action it can perform and assign dice to it. 

When a unit runs out of action dice, it is exhausted. 


activate > roll > prepare >move > act




Shooting:
difficulty 1 per range increment (6")
use range increments of 3" in forests
add 1 to difficulty if target is in cover
add 1 to difficulty if target is down
add 1 to difficulty if target is small
