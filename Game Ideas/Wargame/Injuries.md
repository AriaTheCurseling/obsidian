An injury can be curtailed with a 2+ or cured with a 6

Remove up to 3 different injuries when a model dies

1 - None
2 - Near Miss 
3 - Wound
4 - Leg injury
5 - Arm injury
6 - Head injury
7 - bleeding out
8+ - dead



##### Near Miss
+1 to difficulty of ranged rolls during next activation

##### Wound

\[2+] | Ignore | cures on 6. {free}

##### Leg injury

\[2+] | Ignore | cures on 6. {free}

##### Arm injury

\[2+] | Ignore | cures on 6 {free}

##### Head injury
You cannot perform actions
\[2+] | Ignore | cures on 6 {free}
