
Every decision should feel meaningful
- Options should be hard to compare (orthogonal decision space)
- Options should be impacted by the roll (variation in options)
- All possible rolls must result in impactful decisions
	- Even a "failed" roll should have decisions attached to it

All dice must be assigned
1's can only be added on the character sheet
values below the required get "stuck", called a clogged dice

Rolling a six lets your clear a clogged dice for free, otherwise a clog can be cleared by spending an action dice of the required value. A character cannot have more clogs than action dice, if it would, clear a clog of your choice for free.
