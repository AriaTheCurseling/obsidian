##### Passive - Fuelled by Emotion
>The berserker's motions fuels her abilities, granting them special effects according to her mood. 
>
>Her **Wrath** builds when she is damaged, debuffed or stuned.
>Her **Joy** grows when she deals damage, is healed or is buffed. 
>
>Each emotion can build up to 100, determining her mood:
>
>At half **Wrath** she is **Enraged**
>At half **Joy** she is **Overjoyed**
>If she is both she is **Exhilarated**
>If she is neither she is **Serene**
>
>Using an ability when **Enraged**, **Overjoyed** or **Exhilarated** consumes 50 of the relevant emotion.

##### Q - One Inch Punch
>Deals dmg and applies onhit
> 
>Depending on her mood she:
>>\[**Serene**] launches the punch at range
>>\[**Enraged**] knocks the opponent back
>>\[**Overjoyed**] dashes through the punch
>>\[**Exhilarated**] knocks back and dashes
##### W - Hardened Body / Take the Hit
>Briefly gain CC resist.
> 
>Depending on her mood she also:
>>\[**Serene**] forces target ability to hit her
>>\[**Enraged**] reduces incoming damage
>>\[**Overjoyed**] intercepts projectiles around her
>>\[**Exhilarated**] intercepts projectiles and reduces their damage
##### E - Fancy Footwork 
>Dash a tiny distance 
> 
>Depending on her mood she:
>>\[**Serene**] suspends other abilities
>>\[**Enraged**] 3 small zigzag dashes
>>\[**Overjoyed**] swaps position with enemy and turns them around
>>\[**Exhilarated**] 3 small zigzag swaps turning them each time




---
Joy gives movespeed and cc resist
